## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is it safe to give you my sportsbook password?**  
Yes. We use it once to connect, then immediately delete it. We store only a temporary session token (like staying logged in to Gmail). We can't withdraw your money—sportsbooks require matching bank account names.

**Which sportsbooks do you support?**  
Currently: Bet9ja, SportyBet, 1xBet. We're adding more based on user requests.

**Do you provide betting tips?**  
No. We provide data about YOUR betting. We don't predict games—we help you understand your patterns so you can make better decisions.

**Can I try before paying?**  
Yes. Free tier gives you full access to basic features. Connect your account, see your data, decide if it's worth upgrading.

**What if my sportsbook isn't supported?**  
Email us at support@bettrackr.com. If enough users request it, we'll add it.

**How do you make money?**  
Subscriptions only. We succeed when our tool helps you bet smarter. We don't sell tips, push affiliate links, or sell your data.
