# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 24, 2026**

## Introduction

BetTrackr ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our sports betting analytics platform.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, phone number, full name
- **Sportsbook Credentials**: Username and password for connected sportsbooks (temporarily used, never stored)
- **Payment Information**: Processed through Paystack (we never store full payment card details)

### Information Automatically Collected
- **Betting Data**: Bet history, odds, stakes, results from your connected sportsbook accounts
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with our platform, features used, session duration
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
- **Session Tokens**: Encrypted authentication tokens from sportsbooks (expires after 30 days)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not store your sportsbook passwords
- We do not collect your bank account information
- We do not track your activity on sportsbook websites
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Sync and display your betting history and performance analytics
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Send important service updates and security alerts
- Improve our platform and develop new features
- Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
- Comply with legal obligations

## How We Protect Your Information

### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between you and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
- **Password Protection**: Your sportsbook credentials are used once and immediately deleted
- **Session Tokens**: Encrypted using AES-256 encryption and stored securely
- **Access Control**: Strict internal access controls; only authorized personnel can access user data
- **Regular Audits**: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

### Data Retention
- **Betting Data**: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after deletion
- **Session Tokens**: Automatically expire after 30 days
- **Account Information**: Retained until you request account deletion
- **Sportsbook Passwords**: Never stored; deleted immediately after use

## Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

### Service Providers
- Payment processors (Paystack) for subscription management
- Cloud hosting providers for data storage
- Analytics services to improve our platform

### Legal Requirements
- To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- To detect, prevent, or address fraud or security issues

### With Your Consent
- When you authorize us to share information (e.g., public tipster profiles)
- When you share your betting statistics via our sharing features

### Sportsbook Connections
When you connect a sportsbook account, we access only your bet history. We cannot:

- Modify your account settings

### Payment Processing
Payments are processed by Paystack. We do not store your full credit/debit card information. See Paystack's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your payment data.
